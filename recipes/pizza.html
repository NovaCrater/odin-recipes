Round Table Pizza


Best Pizza in Town

There is no pizza in the world that surpasses that of Round Table. They cook it to perfection everytime.


Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Pepperonis
  • Mushrooms

Steps:

  1. Flatten dough out into circle
  2. Spread pizza sauce
  3. sprinkle cheese
  4. spread pepperonis evenly
  5. put hella mushrooms on
  6. Bake at 400 F for approximately 30 minutes
  7. enjoy
