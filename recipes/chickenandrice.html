Chicken and Rice


Gourmet Giga Chad Meal

This is the perfect meal to meet your dietary needs. Completely bland chicken and rice is the solution to all your problems. It contains all the protein you can need, without any worries of artifical contents due to no type of seasoning or sauce what so ever! The rice is there just because it's rice, rice is awesome. Also no vegetables because we don't need those, protein suffices!


Ingredients

  • As much raw chicken breast as you want
  • White rice

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Bake chicken for approximastely 50 minutes
  3. Pop as much rice as you want into the rice cooker
  4. Bone appetite!
