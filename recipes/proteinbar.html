Giga in a Bar


Best Mid Day Snack

This is the holy grail of foods. It can fit within the palm of your hand, in your pocket, literally anywhere. Tastes amazing too.


Ingredients

  • 300g Protein powder of your choice
  • 150g of oats
  • 90g of dried cranberries
  • 120g of milk
    • ]
  • 100g of peanut butter
  • (Optional)chocolate chips

Steps:

  1. Mix protein powerd, otas, and dried cranberries in a bowl
  2. Microwave milk and peanutbutter for about 30 seconds to a minute and then mix
  3. Mix all ingredients togther in a bowl until able to form a mushy ball
  4. Flatten out into a pan and refrigerate for a couple hours
  5. Put as many chocolate chips in a bowl and microwave for about a minute, then spread evenly over protein mix
  6. Let refrigerate for 30+ more minutes, then you can take out and cut into bars
